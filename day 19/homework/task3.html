Information about Goa

The Goal-Oriented Academy (GOA) is an educational institution based in Georgia that focuses on equipping students with both technical and personal development skills. GOA offers programs in programming, graphic design, and film making, aiming to foster a positive learning environment where students can grow academically and personally.



The Goal-Oriented Academy (GOA), founded by Nika Keshelava, is a Georgian educational institution dedicated to developing both technical skills and personal growth in students. GOA offers a range of programs, including programming, graphic design, and film making, aiming to prepare students for modern business and creative industries.
